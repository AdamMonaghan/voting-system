Online Voting

Welcome to Online Voting. What do you wish to do?

We use GOV.UK Verify. If you do not already have an account, we will ask you to make one before you cast your vote. We advise this is done before the day of elections.

Register to Vote Online

Cast You Vote Online

To caste your vote online, you must first register to vote online. You must complete this process for each election.

You will need a poll card for each election you would like to register for. This will arrive in the mail.

If you have registered to vote online for an election, once the online voting opens, you may use the button below to cast your vote.

You will need the password you created when you registered and a voter code which you would have been sent in the mail once you registered.
