Cast Your Vote Online

In order to cast your vote in an election, you must be eligible to vote, and should be registered to vote on upcoming/existing elections. If you're not registered to vote in any elections, go back to the Online Voting Dashboard on the previous page. The next pages will require you to have your voter details ready. These details are your email, password, voter ID

REMEMBER: You only get to vote once in an election, once you've cast your vote you will not be able to vote in the same election again.
