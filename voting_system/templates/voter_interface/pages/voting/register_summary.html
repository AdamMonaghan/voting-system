Register to Vote Online

To cast your vote online, you must first register to vote online. You must complete this process for each election. You will need a poll card for each election you would like to register for. This will arrive in the mail.

Active Elections

You will select the election you wish to register for later in the process. For now, please make sure that the election you wish to register for is included within the list below. If it is missing, this may be because the online registration process has not yet opened, has closed, or the election is not available for online voting.
